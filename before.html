Current Issues

- inter-agency accident report communication standards

- geographic mis or under representation

- lack of universal data standards

- past partial solutions not industry-standard

G.I.S. Features

- industry-standard spatial format

- supports file formats used by open-source and proprietary software

- employs universal data standard using Local Government Data Standard (LGIM)

- explicit geography (ability to pinpoint event locations)

- supports a variety of outputs for technical and non-technical users, other agencies, the public, and the federal government

Other Benefits

- can promote eco-tourism

- career pathway in and out of transportation industry
